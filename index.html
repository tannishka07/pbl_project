AI-Driven Predictive Inventory & Dynamic Pricing System

Hypothesis / Project Statement:
Can artificial intelligence integrate historical sales data with hyperlocal signals such as weather, regional events, and social media trends to accurately forecast SKU-level demand and autonomously optimize inventory and pricing decisions in real time?

Problem Statement

Large retail chains manage thousands of products across multiple stores. Traditional ERP and POS systems rely primarily on historical sales and fixed reorder rules. They fail to anticipate sudden hyperlocal demand shifts caused by festivals, weather changes, local events, breaking news, or viral trends.

  • Frequent stockouts during demand spikes
  • Overstock during demand drops
  • Revenue loss due to static pricing
  • High holding costs and wastage

Literature Review / Market Research

Research shows AI-based forecasting improves retail efficiency when external signals like weather and local events are included. Dynamic pricing models increase revenue and optimize profit margins. However, most systems treat forecasting and pricing separately.

Research Gap / Innovation

Existing systems lack an integrated framework combining predictive demand sensing, dynamic pricing, automated restocking, and financial simulation. This project develops a unified AI-driven retail optimization engine capable of real-time autonomous decision-making.
