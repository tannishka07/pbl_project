Mental Wellness Application | PBL 2026

मन saathi – Mental Wellness App for Students

Project Statement:
Can a student-centric digital platform integrate mood tracking, music therapy, journaling, relaxation tools, and motivational support to provide private, accessible, and effective mental wellness support for students?

Problem Statement

College students face increasing levels of stress, anxiety, and burnout. There is a need for an integrated, affordable, and student-focused mental wellness platform.

Literature Review / Market Research

Existing apps like Wysa, Calm, and Headspace provide limited or paid solutions. There is no unified student-centric platform.

Research Gap / Innovation

Our platform integrates mood tracking, music therapy, journaling, relaxation tools, and analytics in one student-focused solution.

System Methodology

Input Data

User mood entries and wellness activity data.

System Architecture

Personalized recommendations using mood analysis.

Application Execution

Live demonstration of the platform.

DEMO VERSION

Results & Analysis

Accuracy / Performance 85%

Students reported improved emotional awareness and reduced stress levels.
